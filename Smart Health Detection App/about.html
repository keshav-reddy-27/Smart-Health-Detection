Smart Health Prediction using AI is a groundbreaking mobile application designed to revolutionize the way we approach heart health. 
        Leveraging the power of artificial intelligence, this innovative app aims to provide users with personalized and accurate predictions about their cardiovascular well-being. 
        By analyzing user-generated health data, such as vital signs, activity levels, and medical history, Smart Health Prediction utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to assess and predict potential heart-related risks. 
        This app not only empowers individuals to take proactive measures for a healthier lifestyle but also serves as a proactive companion in the journey towards heart health management.
        
The primary objective of the Smart Health Prediction using AI app is to revolutionize heart health management by harnessing the capabilities of artificial intelligence. 
        This innovative application aims to provide users with personalized predictions and insights into their cardiovascular well-being based on the analysis of comprehensive health data. 
        By incorporating machine learning algorithms, the app strives to offer accurate assessments of potential heart-related risks, enabling users to make informed decisions about their lifestyle and health choices
